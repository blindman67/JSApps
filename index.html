  • Right click in empty space creates a point
  • Right click over point attaches a line
  • Right click over empty space while creating line ends lines and starts a new one
  • Right click over point while creating line attaches line to point and starts a new line
  • Left click empty space to deselect all
  • Left click on line or point adds it to selection
  • Left click hold then drag will move view (Cursor icon will change to move icon)
  • Use mouse wheel to zoom in and out

  • How to play
  • Hold left mouse to aim.
  • Left drag sets direction and power.
  • The further you drag the cue from cue ball the more power in the strike.
  • Release left button to take shot.
  • Fine anim and lock anim.
  • With left button down, hold down right button for fine amining.
  • With left and right button down drag around cue ball to adjust aim clockwise or counterclockwise.
  • Releasing right button while left button down locks strike direction.
  • You can still change strike power with left button down by changing cue distance to que ball.
  • To make a shot release the left button.
  • To cancel a shot. Lock direction [right button while left down]. Drag cue over cue ball and release left button.
  • Taking the shot
  • When you have hit the cue ball the sim will run until all balls have stopped.
  • You can speed up the sim while waiting for balls to stop by holding right button.
  • When all the colored balls have been sunk the table will reset.
  • If you sink the cue ball you will need to place it in the D to resume play.
  • Sunk balls are shown at the bottom of the screen in the order they are sunk.
  • Placement mode.
  • Before taking a shot, right click to enter placement mode.
  • Left click the ball to move.
  • Move the ball to where you want it and left click to put it down.
  • When you want to play select the cue ball and place it where you want to take a shot from.
  • Extras.
  • Hold [W] while taking a shot to add extra power to your shot.
  • Hold [S] while taking a shot to hit the cue ball above center. This will give the ball legs.
  • Hold [X] while taking a shot to hit the cue ball below center. This will add back spin.
